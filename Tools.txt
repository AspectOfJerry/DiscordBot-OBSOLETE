500 INTERNAL_SERVER_ERROR - A generic error message, given when an unexpected condition was encountered and no more specific message is suitable.
501 NOT_IMPLEMENTED - The server either does not recognize the request method, or it lacks the ability to fulfil the request. Usually this implies future availability.
